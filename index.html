🔤 Basic Rules of Reading English

Here are some simple reading rules for English learners:

  • A – sounds like æ in cat, or eɪ in cake.
  • E – sounds like e in pen, or iː in tree.
  • I – sounds like ɪ in sit, or aɪ in five.
  • O – sounds like ɒ in dog, or oʊ in home.
  • U – sounds like ʌ in sun, or juː in student.

👉 Practice these rules by reading simple words every day.
