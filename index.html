🔤 Basic Rules of Reading English

  • A – æ in cat, eɪ in cake.
  • E – e in pen, iː in tree.
  • I – ɪ in sit, aɪ in five.
  • O – ɒ in dog, oʊ in home.
  • U – ʌ in sun, juː in student.

👉 Practice these rules by reading simple words every day.
